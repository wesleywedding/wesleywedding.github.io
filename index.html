Our Story

LOVE AT FIRST SIGHT

Prior to meeting Teddi, I don’t think that I ever would have claimed that love at first sight was a real thing. Even now it seems like too fantastical of a concept to claim as reality, yet at the same time I can’t deny that by all means our story very much follows that pattern.

I can distinctly and vividly remember the Baylor send off party where I first saw Teddi. It was being hosted in Corpus Christi in Summer 2012 and my mom and I drove down to meet other Baylor students and parents. After signing in at the front table I turned around only to have my breath completely taken away by the girl who unbeknownst to me at the time, I would be blessed to call my future wife. Being the dashing and confident teenager I was at the time, I decided to muster up my courage and totally not have a conversation during the night due to the nerves I was feeling. Skip forward through a year of seeing Teddi around campus at dining halls and a whole years worth of trying to muster up the courage to introduce myself and we both ended up at the 2013 Baylor send off party. Not wanting to miss my chance, I finally managed to introduce myself to Teddi. After a pleasant conversation about our times at Baylor we ended up going separate ways and I found myself just as infatuated as I felt a year prior.

Unfortunately for me, our Baylor walks were different enough that our paths didn’t cross with much frequency, which made asking Teddi out without it coming out of the blue pretty much impossible. Eventually though I decided that was my only option and I wanted to find out if the most beautiful girl in the world could possibly have a personality to match (Spoiler alert, she does). While on my phone outside of the Baylor Computer Science building I happened to see Teddi bike up to the nearby residential college that she was a Community Leader in. Not wanting to miss what I was worried might be one of a few moments where I would be lucky enough to be in the same area as her, I quickly ended my call and ran over to the residential college before I could get too nervous to talk myself out of the situation. Although Teddi originally thought that I was trying to “tailgate” into the dorm (which is against campus policy and would have ended with her writing me up) I managed to, in what I can only assume was an incredibly suave manner, ask her to go on a date with me.

As you may have guessed, to my delight she agreed! Despite Teddi’s busy senior year we managed to squeeze in a few incredible dates where we went to the zoo, rooftops and secret waterfalls. All the while realizing that the more I learned about this girl the more smitten I was becoming. Long story short, we spent the next few years dating, growing closer, sharing our favorite TV shows with each other and learning that overall we’re actually pretty fond of the other.

On May 10, 2018 I rented out the location of our very first date, the Cameron Park Zoo. After hours and with candles and lights surrounding the area I asked Teddi to bless me with the opportunity of spending our lives together. As you can tell, she said yes! We are so excited to begin the next chapter of our lives together. I still don’t know how I feel about the concept of love at first sight, but I do know that from the moment I first laid eyes on Teddi I was and continue to be absolutely stunned.
